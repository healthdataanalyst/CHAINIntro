FAQ

Straight answers.
No fine print.

Is this actually free, or will I be upsold constantly?
The self-directed path — courses, weekly webinars, Drive access, learning tracker, recordings, resource lists, WhatsApp community — is free. Always. No upsell, no pressure. Mentoring and group projects are a separate, clearly priced paid option. You will never be asked to pay to access what was promised for free.
I have zero experience. Am I really welcome?
Yes — completely. Zero experience is the default starting point, not an exception. All questions are welcome via WhatsApp or during live Q&A. Ryan answers what he can't cover live as a follow-up. No question is too basic.
How much time does this take each week?
The self-directed path is entirely at your own pace — no required hours, no fixed deadlines. The only structured touchpoint is the weekly webinar (1 hr) + Q&A (30–60 min), recorded if you can't attend live. The mentored path has real deadlines — time commitment is discussed at enrollment based on the track you choose.
How is this different from just watching YouTube?
The free path does point you to YouTube and free courses — the curation and live Q&A with a working professional is what's different. The mentored path is entirely different: you're inside a simulated office on real projects, getting performance feedback, and building a portfolio that speaks for itself in an interview.
I'm in a different timezone. Will I be left out?
No. Every webinar is recorded and shared via the Drive folder immediately after — available to all participants. You can follow the full program entirely async if needed. The WhatsApp community also operates across timezones actively.
Is there a certificate I can show employers?
Yes. Free path participants are eligible for a certificate of completion by passing a hands-on exam held every 4 months — based on real datasets, not trivia. Mentored path participants build a project portfolio that is significantly stronger than any certificate alone.
